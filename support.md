# Faraway Support

Thanks for using Faraway — ambient soundscapes for sleep, focus, relaxation, and calm.

## Contact us

For questions, bug reports, or feedback, email **contact@getfaraway.app**. We reply within 2 business days.

## Common questions

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Open Settings → Manage Subscription, or go to your iPhone's Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Faraway.

**How do I restore a previous purchase?**
In the app: Settings → Restore Purchases.

**How do I delete my account?**
Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data.

**A sound isn't playing or the app is silent.**
Try toggling airplane mode, closing and reopening the app, or force-quitting. If the issue persists, email us with your device model and iOS version.

**I want to request a sound or feature.**
We'd love to hear it — email contact@getfaraway.app.
